Learn More About Us

In our UI, UX designer course you will not only learn the fundamentals of design, but also explore the method of user-centric designing, get hands-on experience of building a site from scratch, work with ideation structures, and so on. You will learn how to do user research and user interviews and create a design that speaks and appeals to them; you’ll learn emergent technologies, and how to combine your creative skills with them to craft stunning web designs. Aviv Digital is a part of Acodez, where we have international award-winning UX design professionals who will provide an immersive learning experience like nowhere else – equipping you to enter the vast sea of opportunities of UX design. The UX designer training provided at Aviv will ensure that you have an edge over other new designers, enabling you to chart a successful career path.
